Problem Statement

The current state of our nonprofit organization's website presents significant challenges for users in effectively navigating and engaging with our content. Users encounter difficulties while attempting to explore the website due to an unintuitive header, awkward scrolling processes on the main page, and a lack of features that facilitate a meaningful connection with our mission. These issues not only hinder the user experience but also impede our ability to effectively communicate our organization's purpose and impact. How might we address these usability and accessibility concerns to ensure that our website becomes a powerful tool for fostering empathy, engagement, and support for our mission among visitors?
